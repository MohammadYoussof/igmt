Hypocentres from the NEIC dataset

Figure 1: ETOPO5, NUVEL1 plate boundaries and PDE hypocenter distribution as of example1.ps, resolution reduced. Data from NOAA (1988); DeMets et al. (1990); USGS/NEIC (1998).

Figure 1 shows the map of example1.ps from the iGMT distribution, the whole Earth in the Mollweide projection. ETOPO5 in 60 arc minute resolution is the ground raster layer. All hypocentres of the USGS/NEIC dataset from 1973 - 1997 with magnitude greater than five and NUVEL1 plate boundaries are superimposed. Load example1.dat to produce this plot. To reduce the size of this documentation, the postscript file is not exactly that produced by iGMT but a converted GIF with lower resolution.
