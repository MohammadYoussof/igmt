Item Region

This item brings up the region selection dialog. Where the eastern, northern etc. boundaries are self-explaining, the ``Center of map projection'' is needed for whole earth viewing projections. Clicking on ``The whole thing!'' expands the geographical boundaries as far as possible for the checked projection. ````Square'' it'' attempts to make a square-like map by setting the difference between the boundaries equal. ``Center'' sets the center of map projection values to the averages of the boundaries and adjusts the reference meridian to the average latitude (needed for some projections). ``0/180 <-> 0/360'' attempts to go from one system of specifying longitudinal ranges to another.
