Documentation and data

The file manual.ps is the manual you are reading as a postscript file. nuvel.yx is the modified plate boundary polygon file after DeMets et al. (1990), 01_02-98.cmt contains the Harvard CMT double couple fault plane solution for the first 60 days of 1998 as an example, vocanoes.dat the volcano locations after Simkin and Siebert (1994), allslabs_rum.gmt the slab seismicity contours of Gudmundsson and Sambridge (1998) and hotspots.dat the hotspot list of Steinberger (2000).
