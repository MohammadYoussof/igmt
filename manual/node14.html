Menu items for pscoast

The first three items deal with pscoast. A small subset of the polygon data that can be plotted by this routine are mentioned in the Pscoast polygon selection list. The next item allows changing the color of the land and sea coverage, while the last pscoast item is responsible for changing some linewidths.
